About Us

Cleaning with care, detail, and consistency.

Prado’s Cleaning LLC is built around dependable service, professional communication, and a high standard of cleanliness.

Built on Experience, Reliability, and Results

Prado’s Cleaning LLC is a family-run business built on professionalism, trust, and attention to detail. As owners, we bring both hands-on cleaning experience and industry insight that helps us deliver results that go beyond surface-level cleaning.

With a background in property management within multi-family housing (MFH), we understand how critical fast, efficient unit turns are. Every day a unit sits vacant is lost revenue, which is why we prioritize reliability, speed, and consistent quality.

We know what property managers are looking for — not just a “clean” unit, but a space that feels ready, fresh, and move-in ready the moment a prospective tenant walks in.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Experience in multi-family property management
  • Understanding of turnover timelines and rental loss
  • Detail-oriented cleaning that goes beyond surface level
  • Focus on both appearance and smell
  • Reliable and scalable for apartment communities

Cleanliness isn’t just visual — it’s sensory. Smell, detail, and presentation all play a role in how a space is perceived. Our team knows what to look for and how to deliver a result that supports occupancy, retention, and overall property value.
